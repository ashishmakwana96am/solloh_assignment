Microneedling

Microneedling

Microneedling helps produce collagen and elasticity to your skin. Additionally, it reduces the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, hyper pigmentation, uneven skin tone, acne scars, traumatic scars and stretch marks.

Halo Laser

Halo Laser

At Austin Plastic Surgery Institute, we strive to offer the most cutting-edge technology. Halo by Sciton is one of the newest lasers being used in the industry to treat a variety of skin conditions. The hybrid fractional laser has been approved by the FDA to treat a variety of conditions, and it erases fine lines and wrinkles, lightens pigmentation, and is effective in treating acne scars.

BBL

BBL

Are you looking for younger, more refreshed and rejuvenated skin? Forever Young BBL is an innovative technology that uniquely delivers broadband light therapy targeting the signs of aging and sun damage.

Mission

The mission of NorthStar Surgery Specialists, P.A. is dedicated to promoting health care by providing excellent emergency service and elective general surgery service to the Austin metro and surrounding areas.

Vision

While starting as a solo practice, the goal will be to eventually add other surgery specialists with the common mission of advancing the practice of surgery. This will be done through being involved with continuing medical education, research, advancements in surgical technology, and involvement in medical teaching with the Austin area medical students.
